Course Overview

This course introduces you to Generative AI, including Large Language Models (LLMs), prompt engineering, fine-tuning, and practical applications in text, images, and code generation. You’ll work with popular frameworks like OpenAI, Hugging Face, and Stable Diffusion while learning both theory and hands-on implementation.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, you'll understand how generative AI models work, apply prompt engineering effectively, fine-tune pre-trained models, and build AI-powered applications. You’ll also gain practical experience deploying generative AI projects and earn a certificate to showcase your expertise.

Target Audience

This course is for developers, data scientists, entrepreneurs, and tech enthusiasts who want to explore the power of Generative AI. A basic understanding of Python is helpful but not mandatory.

Course Duration

The course is structured over 8 weeks. Each week includes video lessons, coding labs, quizzes, and project assignments. Learn at your own pace while engaging in interactive sessions.
