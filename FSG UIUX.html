Course Overview

This course takes you from the fundamentals of graphic design to advanced UI/UX workflows. You’ll learn color theory, typography, branding, and visual storytelling, while mastering design tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma, and Adobe XD. You’ll create responsive web and mobile designs with a focus on usability and creativity.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, you’ll be able to design logos, posters, and digital artwork, craft engaging UI/UX prototypes, build wireframes, and design interactive user flows. You’ll graduate with a professional design portfolio and certification to showcase your skills.

Target Audience

This course is ideal for aspiring graphic designers, UI/UX enthusiasts, freelancers, entrepreneurs, and students who want to build a career in design. No prior experience is required — beginners are welcome.

Course Duration

The course runs for 12 weeks and combines recorded video lessons, design challenges, quizzes, and a final design project. Learn at your own pace while joining weekly creative workshops.

Enrollment Fee

The total fee for the course is PKR 3650. This includes design resources, templates, live sessions, and certification. A 30-day money-back guarantee is also available.

Instructor Profile

The lead instructor is a professional graphic designer and UI/UX consultant with over 8 years of experience working with startups and global brands. They bring industry insights, creative workflows, and practical design strategies to help you grow as a designer.

Student Testimonials

Students appreciate how this course blends creative design principles with practical UI/UX skills. Many graduates have landed freelance projects, internships, and full-time design jobs thanks to their professional portfolios developed during the course.
